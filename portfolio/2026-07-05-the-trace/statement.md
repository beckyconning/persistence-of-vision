# The Trace

Eighty-two lines in a journal, drawn in the order they were written. Each strand
is one agent: it enters at its real `started` event, holds a lane while it
works, and drops its result to the spine at its real `result` event. The combs
are phases; the ragged overlaps are the scheduler's honesty — a verifier
starting before the last researcher has finished, exactly as the log says it
happened.

Nothing here is composed. The Room arranged forty-one voices by hand; The Trace
lets the run arrange itself. The named debt from the last piece — crossings
derived from real event order — is paid: every entry, exit, and lane shift is a
line in journal.jsonl of run wf_6e9a1549, the writers' room that invented
Averline Medical.

The last strand is the fixer, event 80 to 81, alone with the spine. Then the
episode returns.
