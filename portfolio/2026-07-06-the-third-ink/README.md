## Self-critique ritual

**1. What did I set out to make?**
The C×M×Y extension of multiply-as-proof: 3-way conjunction, 7 regions,
wordless but for figures.

**2. What actually worked?**
Rectangles instead of Venn circles — the offset-plate geometry reads as
print-shop, not textbook. The subtractive secondaries do real semantic work:
blue/green/red name the pairs without labels, and the near-black center is
both the color-theory endpoint and the meaning ("data flows here"). The
figures carry the whole narrative in five glyphs.

**3. What failed or fell short?**
Composition sits low; the top third is bare paper (margin reads intentional,
but it's accident). The zeros' placement is arithmetic, not optical — the
C×M zero crowds the region edge. The piece leans on the previous piece's
grammar; it's a strong second verse, not a new song.

**4. What does the corpus gain?**
The multiply-as-proof grammar now covers n-ary conjunction; near-black is
established as "all constraints bind". Two-piece series (the-overprint /
the-third-ink) demonstrates the grammar generalizes.

**5. Frontier for next time**

geometry (the-overprint plate 3 hinted), the polyrhythm room (s23), or
simulation-emergent depth. Also: a piece where the DATA chooses the
