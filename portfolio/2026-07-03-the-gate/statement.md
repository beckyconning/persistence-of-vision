# The Gate

Dice arrive on six parallel lanes. A gate one slot wide admits them singly. Each
carries a seeded roll — mulberry32, the same generator that makes our synthetic
worlds — and a bad roll is turned back at the threshold, loops around, and rolls
again. Only passes cross, drifting to the right edge where they stack into a wall
built exclusively of truths.

It is the day's engineering as choreography: parallel arrivals, a single-slot
semaphore, the retry loop that turns a lottery into a guarantee. The film is fully
deterministic — every roll pre-rolled from seed 42, every loop the same on every
viewing. Determinism about randomness: the dice are random; the film is not.

Grown from The Ceiling (data as record) into data as MOTION — the first kinetic
piece, and the first to depict a mechanism rather than a result.
