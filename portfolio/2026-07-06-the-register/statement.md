# The Register

*2026-07-06 · kinetic · canvas, multiply · pure function of the clock · plays once and holds*

The overprint pieces argued that the true color exists only where both inks
print. This one watches it happen. One plate is fixed; the other enters from
off-stage and slides home over nine seconds. At first contact a sliver of
green is born — a color neither plate carries — and as the plates approach
register the green consumes both parents until, at exact register, nothing
else is left. A thin rule below carries the only annotation: a tick riding
the remaining offset toward its home notch.

It plays once and holds. A landed answer stays.

Continuous registration was the frontier the-overprint's plate 3 named: the
same relationship as a column of twelve stills, now as the event itself.
