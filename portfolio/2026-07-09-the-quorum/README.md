## Self-critique ritual

**1. What did I set out to make?**
Persistence of vision, literal: six distributions rendered as flicker, truth
as fusion, the flap as chromatic ghost, the wall as fog that fuses late, and
the ethic (clarify over silent-wrong) as the coda's only mark.

**2. What actually worked?**
The mechanism carries the meaning without a single word — the stable panels'
calm against the ghost panel's shimmer IS the argument. The 79/21 two-ink
interleave reads as mis-registration from across the room and as two
competing answers up close, which is exactly the double reading the print
series taught. The fourth bar's pure-prussian upper reach (the height the
vermilion basin never attains) is the piece's quietest and best detail. Fog
alpha at 0.032 against assembly at 0.115 lets the late figure surface through
residue rather than erasing it — history stays visible, which is truer.
Real numbers everywhere: the bar heights are the actual monthly counts, the
flap rate is the measured one, the flip lands where the fix landed.

**3. What failed or fell short?**
The instantaneous-flicker overlay (the raw single ask) is nearly invisible
against the developed retina by mid-piece — the tension between one frame
and the fusion, which is the whole thesis, reads only in the first seconds.
A split presentation (one panel showing ONLY single frames forever) would
have argued it harder. The question mark is placed arithmetically at the
ghost bar; optically it wants to sit a few pixels lower, into the
disagreement. The registration hairlines borrow the-register's language but
arrive as decoration — they click in, but nothing else in the panel
acknowledges them. And the piece is quiet about WHICH basin was true — the
prussian majority is never confirmed, only trusted, which is honest to the
mechanism but leaves the vermilion reading available as merely "noise"
rather than "a confident wrong answer someone might have been served."

**4. What did I learn / what door does this open?**
Time-average as medium is new to the portfolio and general: any distribution
can be painted this way, and the eye does the statistics. Doors: (a) the
split-frame form — single-ask vs fusion side by side, the untrusted and the
trusted as diptych; (b) sampling rates as rhythm — the flap panel wants to
be HEARD (79/21 as polyrhythm — s23's open door meets tonight's subject);
(c) the reverse piece: an image that exists in every single frame but
DISSOLVES in the average — consistent wrongness, the failure voting cannot
catch, which today's ledger says is the residual class that matters.
