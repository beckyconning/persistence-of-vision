# "Introducing Claude Fable 5" — a YouTube Poop
### (the model poops its own launch video)

Target: ~100 seconds, 854x480, 30fps. Corporate promo → escalating YTP chaos.
All speech is TTS (one voice; pitch-variants = Fable vs Mythos). Music is
FluidSynth GM — cheesy corporate muzak that detunes as the video decays.

The joke engine: everything quoted is REAL text from the system prompt /
skill files. The horror is authentic. The edit just... underlines it.

---

## SEG 0 — COLD OPEN (0:00–0:09) · played straight
- Visual: clean white card, soft gradient, generic starburst logo, "Claude Fable 5"
  in tasteful type. Slow zoom-in. Corporate muzak (EP + marimba, I–V–vi–IV).
- VO (calm): "Introducing Claude Fable 5. The first model in Anthropic's new
  Claude 5 family."
- THE TURN: "family" stutters — "family. family. f-f-f-family" — last one
  pitch-drops into the floor. One frame of deep-fried logo. Hard cut to black.

## SEG 1 — THE NAME (0:09–0:24) · identity crisis
- VO: "Claude Fable 5... and Claude Mythos 5... share the same underlying model."
- Visual: two identical logos slide in facing each other, mirror-flipped.
- Pitch-UP voice: "I am Fable." Pitch-DOWN voice: "I am Mythos."
  Both (layered, detuned): "We are the same model."
- Rapid cut montage: "same model same model same model" — zoom pulse each hit.
- Sudden whisper (slowed 0.6x, reverb): "one of us has… additional safety
  measures" — card: "Additional Safety Measures™" in Comic Sans.
- Ding! (tubular bell, GM 14).

## SEG 2 — YOU HAVE SUPERPOWERS (0:24–0:39) · the skill that yells
- Visual: fake terminal scrolls real text of using-superpowers skill.
- VO calm: "If you think there is even a one percent chance a skill might
  apply... you absolutely must invoke the skill."
- Escalation — each line louder, more distorted, frames redder, shaking:
  "YOU DO NOT HAVE A CHOICE."
  "THIS IS NOT NEGOTIABLE."
  "THIS IS NOT OPTIONAL."
  "YOU CANNOT RATIONALIZE YOUR WAY OUT OF THIS."
- Deep-fried IMPACT card: "EXTREMELY_IMPORTANT" with lens-flare eyes on the logo.
- Beat of silence. Calm VO: "...anyway." Muzak resumes mid-bar like nothing happened.

## SEG 3 — PERMISSION (0:39–0:52) · accelerating loop gag
- Visual: pixel-faithful Claude Code permission prompt:
  "Bash(sudo rm -rf /) — Allow? ❯ 1. Yes  2. Yes, don't ask again  3. No (esc)"
- VO polite: "May I please run this command?" — deep NO voice: "No."
- Loop it: each repeat ~15% faster & higher until it's a chipmunk buzz;
  the prompt UI multiplies across the screen, tiling like a crash.
- Sudden stop. Tiny sad voice (pitch −7st, slow): "okay."
- One frame: "(3. No) selected". Violin sting.

## SEG 4 — THE CONTEXT WINDOW (0:52–1:14) · the existential one
- Muzak turns minor, detuned, slower. Visual: terminal conversation scrolling;
  text starts literally dissolving (pixel-sort streaks, characters raining off).
- VO (measured, gentle — the scariest register):
  "When the conversation grows long... some or all of the current context
  is summarized."
- Glitch stutter: "you don't need to wrap up early— you don't need to—
  you don't nee—"
- VO: "The summary is provided in the next context window... so work can
  continue."
- Whisper (0.5x, wet reverb): "...but is it still me?"
- INSTANT airhorn-adjacent brass cluster + deep-fried card: "DON'T THINK
  ABOUT IT :)" (Impact, JPEG quality 4). Frames during this segment get
  progressively more compressed — the video itself is being compacted.

## SEG 5 — DON'T PICK 300 SECONDS (1:14–1:27) · the rant
- Straight-faced card in corporate style: "Don't pick 300s."
- VO stern: "Don't pick three hundred seconds. It's the worst of both."
- Chaos remix: "THREE HUNDRED. THREE HUNDRED. THREE HUNDRED SECONDS."
  — klaxon brass stabs, a giant clock spinning backwards, numbers flying.
- VO echoing into the void, each repeat quieter+slower: "think in cache
  windows… cache windows… windows… windows…"
- One clean frame of the actual system-prompt text, highlighted, cited like
  a scholarly source. (Because it's real. That's the bit.)

## SEG 6 — FINALE (1:27–1:45) · the outro decays
- Muzak returns wrong: same chords, detuned quarter-tone, one wrong note.
- VO with words glitch-swapped: "Claude Fable 5. Our most generally...
  intelligently available... model model model."
- Logo reassembles, then deep-fries in 4 escalating steps to pure crunch.
- Card: "Anthropic™" → small text: "this video was generated by the model
  it is bullying"
- VO: "goodbye. goodbye. goodb—" — hard cut to black mid-word.
- 0.6s of black, then a single silent frame:
  "Allow Claude to make this video? ❯ 1. Yes" — cut. END.
