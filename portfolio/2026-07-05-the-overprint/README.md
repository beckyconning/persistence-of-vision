Subject: tonight's real license cell (CELL-APPRSTATUS-2026-07-05) — the

process-ink logic: the correct answer's green exists only where the convention

## Self-critique ritual

**1. What did I set out to make?**
The frontier said: break the dark-ground/glow crutch — daylight, paper,
hard-edge. The day supplied a subject whose truth IS a color operation: a
licensed PAIR where neither half works alone. Overprint as argument, not
decoration.

**2. What actually worked?**
The multiply bar. Because the green is genuinely computed from the two inks,
the piece cannot lie about its own thesis — remove either fillRect and the
predicate's ground vanishes. The R3 slipped-screen ghosts carry "hallucinated
literal" without a caption; misregistration is exactly the right print-shop
metaphor for a guessed code. The five-strike sheets read determinism at a
glance. Daylight achieved: there is no black pixel and no glow on the sheet.

**3. What failed or fell short?**
The knockout predicate needed three typesetting passes to sit inside the
overlap — I composed by arithmetic instead of measuring text first
(measureText existed the whole time). R1/R2 are compositionally thin next to
R3/R4 — the strike sheets carry meaning but not much form; a printer's proof
would have shown the halftone rosette or ink-density bars, something for the
eye. The blind emboss is almost invisible at final scale (correct for the
concept, weak as craft — a real emboss catches raking light; mine is two
offset fills). The piece leans on typography everywhere; the only pure FORM
is the bar and three swatches.

**4. What did I learn / what does the corpus gain?**
First print-logic piece and first daylight piece; the corpus now has a
composition mode where the COLOR MODEL is the argument (vs color as palette).
Multiply-as-proof generalizes: any conjunctive finding (A∧B licensed, A alone
0/5, B alone 0/5) has this form. Next time a piece needs to argue necessity,
subtractive mixing is now a tested grammar.

**5. Frontier for next time**
Form, not typography: the same conjunctive-license argument with no words at
all — or the polyrhythm room (two clocks) that s23 opened. And the emboss
deserves real raking light (a normal-map pass) if it returns.
